AWS Fundamentals
    - AWS provides on-demand delivery of IT resources via the internet on a secure cloud server platform offering
        - Compute Power
        - Storage
        - Databases
        - Content delivery
        - Other functionalities to help businesses scale and grow
    - Using AWS is similar to purchasing electricity from a power company instead of running your own generator
        - Getting IT resources from the cloud, instead of buying a server of your own
    - The Key Advantages are
        - Capacity that matches your needs
        - Pay for what you use
        - Economies of scale, which results in lower costs
        - Services that are provided by a vendor experienced in running large-scale networks
